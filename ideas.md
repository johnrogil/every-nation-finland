### Approach A: Nordic Modernism + Bold Mission
**Design Movement:** Scandinavian Modernism meets Bold Evangelical Energy
**Core Principles:**
- Clean white space with powerful typographic hierarchy
- Deep navy and electric blue as primary palette — faith meets Nordic sky
- Asymmetric grid layouts with strong diagonal energy
- Photography-forward with full-bleed hero imagery

**Color Philosophy:** Deep navy (#0A1628) as the anchor of authority and depth; electric blue (#1A56DB) as the brand color of Every Nation; white for clarity and openness; warm gold (#F59E0B) as an accent for calls-to-action. The palette evokes the Finnish sky at midnight — deep, vast, and luminous.

**Layout Paradigm:** Offset grid with large typographic anchors. Hero sections use full-bleed imagery with left-aligned text. City cards use a masonry-style grid with hover reveals. Navigation is a sticky top bar with the globe logo.

**Signature Elements:**
- Large serif display type (Playfair Display) contrasted with clean sans-serif (DM Sans) body
- Globe/world motif from Every Nation branding
- Thin horizontal rule accents in gold

**Interaction Philosophy:** Purposeful hover states with upward lifts and color transitions. Smooth scroll with section fade-ins. City cards expand on hover to reveal location details.

**Animation:** Entrance animations using fade-up on scroll. Hero text uses a staggered word-by-word reveal. City cards have a subtle scale transform on hover.

**Typography System:**
- Display: Playfair Display (bold, italic for emphasis)
- Body: DM Sans (regular 400, medium 500)
- Accent: DM Mono for small labels

### Approach B: Raw Gospel Energy — Editorial Brutalism
**Design Movement:** Editorial Brutalism with Sacred Warmth
**Core Principles:**
- High-contrast black and white with strategic color punches
- Oversized typography as the primary visual element
- Intentional imperfection — slightly irregular grids, bold borders
- Warm off-white backgrounds instead of pure white

**Color Philosophy:** Near-black (#111111) and warm cream (#F5F0E8) as the base, with Every Nation blue (#1A56DB) used sparingly as a high-impact accent. The palette references Finnish design heritage — honest, unadorned, purposeful.

**Layout Paradigm:** Full-width typographic sections. City names displayed as massive oversized text that bleeds off the viewport. Navigation uses a minimal hamburger menu with a full-screen overlay.

**Signature Elements:**
- Massive display numerals for statistics (e.g., "12 Cities")
- Thick black borders and dividers
- Uppercase tracking-widest labels

**Interaction Philosophy:** Bold, confident interactions. Hover states invert colors (black becomes white). Cursor changes to crosshair on interactive elements.

**Animation:** Fast, snappy transitions. No easing — linear transforms that feel decisive. Section transitions use horizontal wipes.

**Typography System:**
- Display: Space Grotesk (extra-bold 800)
- Body: Space Grotesk (regular 400)
- Mono accents: Space Mono

**Design Movement:** Sacred Minimalism with Finnish Light
**Core Principles:**
- Light as metaphor — luminous whites, soft glows, gentle gradients

- Soft blue gradient overlays on hero images

- Display: Fraunces (variable, italic for taglines)

## Selected Approach: **A — Nordic Modernism + Bold Mission**

Deep navy and electric blue palette, Playfair Display + DM Sans typography, asymmetric grid layout with full-bleed photography, globe motif, and purposeful animations. This approach best captures Every Nation's bold global mission while honoring Finnish design sensibility.
